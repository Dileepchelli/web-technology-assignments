MAHINDRA THAR ROXX

The Mahindra Thar ROXX is a 5-door lifestyle SUV that mixes rugged looks with modern comfort. It comes with powerful petrol or diesel engines, manual or automatic gearbox, and optional 4x4 drive. Big tyres, high ground clearance, strong ladder-frame chassis and terrain modes make it perfect for off-road trails, mud, sand or snow. Inside, it offers a touchscreen infotainment system, digital cluster, sunroof, ventilated seats, ADAS safety tech and six airbags. Because it is both tough and family-friendly, the Thar ROXX is one of the most popular choices for adventure lovers in India.
